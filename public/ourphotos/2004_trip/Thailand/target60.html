Still on Ko Pha Ngan--Bottle Beach

We were on the wicked boat tour. We thought we were getting a different tour but it turned out there was only one--the wicked tour. This is where they take you to some beaches around the island while serving pot to the willing participants. Needless to say the age of the people that came along was around 19.
